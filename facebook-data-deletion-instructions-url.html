How to delete a Sadie Active's account

According to Facebook policy, we have to provide the User Data Deletion Callback URL or Data Deletion Instructions URL.

If you want to delete your account from Safie Active App, you can remove it by following these steps:

  1. Go to Profile
  2. Scroll to Delete Account and press on it
  3. Upon pressing you will be asked for confirmation on the delete account popup. Press on Delete account to erase your account from our database

Facebook Login Data Deletion Instructions

  1. Go to your Facebook Account’s “Settings & Privacy”
  2. Click “Settings”
  3. Then click “Apps and Websites”
  4. Search and Click “Sadie Active”
  5. Click “Remove”
